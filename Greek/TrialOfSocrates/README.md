## Notes on I.F Stone's The Trial Of Socrates

```ascii
In unraveling the long-hidden issues of the most famous free speech case of all time, noted author 
I.F. Stone ranges far and wide over Roman as well as Greek history to present an engaging and 
rewarding introduction to classical antiquity and its relevance to society today. 
The New York Times called this national best-seller an "intellectual thriller."
```
